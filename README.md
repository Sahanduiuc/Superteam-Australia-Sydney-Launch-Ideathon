# Superteam-Australia-Sydney-Launch-Ideathon
Ideathon for a startup for Superteam


Title : Voice-based Agentic AI for getting tasks done for the Expats, Travelers, Topurists, Visitors,.... to other countries

Problem : In a global system, people travel to cities outside their country, increasingly more often for work, travel, vacation, ... . It is a one-world system. But there are many problems : Time, Language, unfamiliarity with the retail businesses in the destination country. Think of an Australian needing to pick his son up, buy him soccer boots and drop him at a football field in Tokyo , while very busy at work. Think of an Australian couple traveling in countryside Thailand and suddenly the man gettinga  heart attack , no knowledge of thai language, no knowledge of heart surgery departments. Think of an australian family traveling to Czech republic for their nephew's wedding and they suddenly have to order flowers and suits and cabs and... in Czech republic!!!

Your idea : The idea is to create a voice-based app , that receives the voice of the user, based on the context and meaning , decides to connect with the relevant businesses in the host country, to get the task done in the best possible way ( Quality , Cost, Speed , ...) . The first step is the simple tasks. The next step is for the complex tasks such as : " I need to attend the funeral of my best friend in Prague's cathedral tomorrow. do all the arrangements from attire to flowers and rides"

Why now : AI voice processing , Agentic AI , Translation are at their peak and have reached maturity! LLMs and Agentic AI are the most potent ever and can also break complex tasks into mini tasks and execute!

How it could work, light execution plan : A SOTA voice-to-text , converts the voice to text , then the text is fed into an Agentic AI , that understands the context, breaks the large task into subtasks , which they will then delegate it to various agents to be executed for the best possible execution!
